Water Jacket Type Hydro Testing Machine

Designed for precise volumetric expansion testing of CNG cylinders with world-class accuracy.

The Water Jacket Type Hydro Testing Machine is a specialized piece of equipment that measures both permanent and elastic expansion of a cylinder under controlled pressure conditions. This method is the most reliable way to ensure that high-pressure cylinders remain safe and compliant with international standards.

Key Features

  • Leak-proof water jacket chamber for reliable data

  • Automatic pressure control and monitoring system
