Industrial Oil Removal Unit

Highly effective system for cleaning internal oil residues and contaminants from CNG and industrial cylinders.

Oil accumulation inside high-pressure cylinders can compromise safety and gas purity. Our Industrial Oil Removal Units use specialized solvent circulating and heating technology to thoroughly dissolve and remove oily residues. This process is essential for maintaining the volumetric accuracy of hydrotesting and ensuring the longevity of cylinder life.

Key Features

  • High-pressure solvent spray nozzle system
  • Integrated heating element for better oil dissolution
  • Solvent filtration and recycling unit
  • Adjustable cleaning cycles for different contamination levels
  • Safe handling and disposal of waste oil
