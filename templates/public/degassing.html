Industrial Degassing Unit

Essential safety equipment designed for removing residual gases from CNG cylinders before maintenance.

Our Industrial Degassing Units are critical for safety in any cylinder testing and maintenance facility. These units ensure that all flammable or hazardous gases are effectively evacuated from cylinders, making them safe for internal inspection, testing, and valve replacement. Engineered with spark-proof components and high-efficiency vacuum systems.

Key Features

  • High-capacity vacuum pump for rapid evacuation
  • Spark-proof motor and electrical configuration
  • Multi-cylinder connection manifold system
  • Integrated pressure and vacuum gauges
  • Mobile or stationary design options
